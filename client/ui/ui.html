Delivery Driver

Do you think you have what it takes to make these deliveries on time, partner? We need someone who can drive quickly and safely. Someone who will make our deliveries on time.

If you think you're the one for the job, go ahead and accept the contract below.

Our Offer:
- We will pay driver $2.00 per 5 minutes of work.
- We will allow driver to keep all tips from customers.
- We will provide one small cart and horse for driver.
- If said horse and cart are not returned when quitting the job, the driver will be charged a minimum of $100.00.
- The driver will be required to make each delivery within 10 minutes time or they will be fired.

Do you agree to these terms?

Doctor

Our Offer:
- We will pay you $3.00 per 5 minutes of work.
- You will be required and under oath to treat every wounded and sick person you come across.
- You will be provided medical supplies to treat your patients.
- If you refuse to treat any wounded or sick person you are subject to arrest.

Do you agree to these terms?

- You can keep and sell all of the bodies and pelts.
- We will not provide any weapons or ammo.

Do you agree to these terms?
